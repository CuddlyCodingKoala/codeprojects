Feed Will Scroll Here

Includes:

  • Posts of each life event/memory.
  • Posts w/ video automatically play when you hover over them.
  • Picture posts w/ multiple images display a scrolling preview when you hover.

Profile Options

Includes:

  • Create posts that are either written memories, videos, live feeds, single photos, or collages.
  • Can also create 3-D "experiences"-- users can use the app to create a 3-d image of an environment (e.g., a room); photo editor in the app stitches photos together to make a 3-D look and create depth. Users then explore the environment by hovering their cursor over the direction they want to look in.
  • Written memories = blog posts; they can either be displayed on a person's profile and added to the feed, and/or shared on forums the user wants.

  • Can cross-link with like-minded groups
